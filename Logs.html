The Art of Timeline Reconstruction

In modern cybersecurity, responding to a breach is only the beginning. One of the most critical skill for a Cybersecurity Analyst is the ability to reconstruct the Kill Chain—the exact sequence of events that allowed an adversary to penetrate the network. Establishing a clear timeline is essential for legal evidence, identifying the root cause, and ensuring the same vulnerability is not exploited twice.

This task requires more than just technical knowledge; it demands critical thinking to differentiate between malicious intent and standard system noise, pattern recognition to spot the signs of lateral movement, and attention to detail.
